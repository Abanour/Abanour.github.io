Explore Central London's Food and Market

Central London is home to many types of food from all across the world and also served in different ways. you will find your traditional restaurant style serving but if you want to experience living in London then there are many markets for you to try out all across central London and London itself. Here are a couple places you could visit and remember to explore to find more.

Borough Market

Borough Market is a wonderful food market hidden near London Bridge station not easy to find... It took me 10 minutes of wondering around to find this hidden gem. Once found, all the time it took to find the market will be forgotten.... The wonderful smell of food can be smelled as soon as you go down some steep steps from London Bridge Station. The market is full of different food from sausage rolls, falafel wraps and sweet and savoury food and way more.

Chinatown gate

Chinatown is composed of 3 long street. Each featuring a beautifully crafter gate which is enough for tourists to stop and take pictures but that is not all it holds... The town is full of food ranging from sweet to main meals and restaurant built in a Chinese theme as though you were in China experiencing the food and culture side by side.
