Explore Central London's Musuems and Gallery

London and the world are full of many history and wonders. There are buildings which hold these wonders and history which you can visit. Different museums and galleries ranging from war, art and much more. As it has been mentioned befor; there will be museums and galleries which have not been mentioned and it is your job to explore and find them. Musuems or galleries which are free of charge will be clearly shown.

Museums

British Museum

British Museum a perfect environment for families and an opportunity to learn about the old kings and civilisations. The museum exhibitions change every once in while for new exhibitions so keep a watch out for any changes on British Museum website

Museum of London

Museum of London exhibits art pieces and art installations dating back since 450,000 BC till today but not all at once. There are different exhibition and that would mean to keep an eye on the Museum of London website for any changes made to the art and the art installations.

Galleries

Tate Modern

Tate Modern is a gallery exhibiting modern, contemporary art and some art installation. Walk about and enjoy the art. After going through the enatrance, you will be met by a massive spacious area with a slope. Just like in any museum or gallery, the art changes overtime so make sure to visit Tate Modern website to keep up to date with the different arts.

The National Gallery

The National Gallery is an exhibition of masterpieces by masterpieces. If you dont know about any of the art... don't worry, there are daily guided tour to allow you to get the most out of the visit. There is also a virtual tour on The National Gallery's website for you to experience before going to the gallery because it does get crowded. And after you had a look, you can enjoy sitting on the steps facing Trafalgar Square.

Hayward Gallery

Hayward gallery is a place to see contemporary art and some some art installations. Check Hayward Gallery website to keep up to date with the different arts installations.

Bankside Gallery

Bankside is a gallery full of art and mini painting. If you are hands on then this is the place to visit because of the shows which are planned all throughout the year. Visit Bankside Gallery's website to keep updated with any show and events held.
